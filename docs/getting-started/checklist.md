# Quick-start checklist

> Everything you need to go from unboxing to your first seed in a few minutes.

Before you power on your SeedSigner device for the first time, make sure you have everything ready.

## What you need

- **An assembled SeedSigner device** — If you haven't built yours yet, head over to the [Hardware Build Guide](/hardware-build/components.md) first.
- **A microSD card with SeedSigner software flashed** — If you haven't done this step, follow the [Software Setup guide](/software-setup/download-and-verify.md).
- **A micro USB cable**
- **A power source** — Any standard USB power bank, phone charger, or computer USB port works fine.

> **Tip:** You do not need an internet connection. SeedSigner is designed to stay completely offline.

## Get started in four steps

1. **Plug in power.** Connect the micro USB cable to the power-only port on your SeedSigner (the port nearest the joystick) and to your power source.
2. **Wait for boot.** The device takes a few seconds to start up. You will see the SeedSigner splash screen.
3. **Dismiss the SD card message.** Press any button (A, B, or C) to clear the "You can remove the SD card now" notification.
4. **You're at the main menu.** That's it — your SeedSigner is ready to use.

> **Tip:** For maximum security, you can physically remove the microSD card after the boot message appears. The operating system runs entirely from RAM, so the device continues to work normally without the card inserted. This guarantees no private data can ever be written to storage.
